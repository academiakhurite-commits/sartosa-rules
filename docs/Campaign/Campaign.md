# Campaign

The main objective of the campaign players is to become the new Pirate King of Sartosa.  

To decide who will be the next Pirate King there will be a vote after a certain amount of rounds.  

Every player must cast a vote. The players can vote for themselves or other pirates.  
Players that control a major port can cast one additional vote.  
The player that controls the Pirate king fortress can cast 2 additional votes.  

The Pirates with the most votes will fight in a final battle.  

Apart of the players votes, different and respectable pirates will cast their vote based on the following:  

**Captain Jack “Blackwake” Crowe**  
He loves gold and treasures over anything, he will cast his vote for the pirate that has more gold by the time of the voting. He can't wait for him to die to start to search for his treasure.

**Maribel “Red Mercy” Vance**  
Daughter of a once respectable bretonian admiral, will cast her vote to the pirate warband with the least amount of routs.

**Tobias “Saltfang” Rourke**  
Oldest of all human pirates on the island, will cast his vote to the pirate captain with the highest amount of experience.

**Gideon “Lantern Jack” Hollow**  
Once a Tilean cartographer, nowadays spends his days in Roba. He will cast his vote to the pirate who has exploded most territories.

**Helena “Gravewind” the Amazon**  
No one really dares to get close to Helena, getting close to her zone is a guaranteed blood bath. She will cast her vote for the pirate warband with the most permanent kills.

**Barundr “Krakenseeker” Skalfsson**  
This old dwarf only respects those who have killed at least one sea beast. He will cast his vote for the pirate with the most monster kills(Kraken rest or Giant Crab).

**Zingag “Goldtongue”**  
Orc chieftain of Osso Hills will vote to whoever put him out of action (Osso Hills territory) in case of more than one beat him they must duel each other for his vote.

**Roderick “Chainshot” Nuln**  
Known for his dealings with the Dark Elves. He will take any Swabbies offered to him. In the vote, he will vote for the pirate who has given him the most Swabbies. After capturing a Swabbie, you can choose to keep it or offer it to Roderick. For each Swabbie given, you will have a -1 penalty on the 2d6 roll to obtain Swabbies.
